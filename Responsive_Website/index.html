The short story of how we came to have this cat was one of slight impulse. Our landlords are completely against dogs and so we always had a thought of maybe getting a cat. This is where I come in. I have never liked cats, never wanted a cat, and thought I would never own a cat in my life. One thing led to another and my wife conviced me to go to Animal Ark one night to just go check out what cats they had. We got there and they told us that they usually only have cats in the spring and that they got a random kitty from a breeder and it was their only one. She let me hold the cat and one thing led to another and I became a cat owner/lover.

Fun Facts About Our Cat

  • Her name is Kit Kat
  • She loves snuggling
  • Does NOT like car rides
  • Enjoys herself a salmon treat
  • The tails are massacred on all of her mouse toys
  • Being alone is worse than death for her
  • She smells like a dryer sheet, despite never being in contact with them (to our knowledge)
  • When we say "Fast Kitty" she goes into crazy mode and runs and jumps on everything
